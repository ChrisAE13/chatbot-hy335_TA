Ah, prefix lists, the gatekeepers of IP addresses. It's like having a bouncer at the door of a club, deciding who's in and who's out. Now, let's dissect this concept.

Prefix lists are a set of rules that dictate which IP prefixes are allowed or denied. In simpler terms, they're like a VIP list for IP addresses. You decide who gets the red carpet treatment and who's stuck in the general admission line.
